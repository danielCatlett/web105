The Room: The Citizen Kane of Movies?

Home | Review | Critical Reception | Watch

Is this the greatest movie of all time? Yes, yes it is.

When talking about the greatest movies of all time, there are plenty of standard candidates. Although the likes of The Godfather, Pulp Fiction, and Space Jam are often rightfully included, there is one movie that trumps them all. In fact, I would say that it is better than all of those movies, combined. So I felt it necessary to dedicate this site to the greatest work of all time and the pinnacle of human achievement, Tommy Wiseau's The Room.
